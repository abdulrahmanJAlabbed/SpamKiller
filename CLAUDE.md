## Design Context

### Users
Primary users are those seeking a **Silent Protector**. They want to install the app and feel immediate **Relief**, trusting that the app is **Taking Care Of** their security without constant interruptions. They value intelligence and autonomy.

### Brand Personality
*   **Relief**: The interface should feel like a deep breath—calm, stable, and reassuring.
*   **Smart**: Minimalist but sophisticated, using intelligent defaults rather than complex configurations.
*   **Protective**: Secure and trustworthy, inspired by the industrial-yet-clean aesthetic of **NordVPN**.

### Aesthetic Direction
*   **Theme**: Moving away from generic "AI" purple glassmorphism towards a more "Industrial-Refined" look.
*   **Inspiration**: **NordVPN**. Clean lines, deep tinted dark modes (not pure black), and clear, meaningful status indicators.
*   **Adaptability**: The design must adapt gracefully across contexts, feeling like a native "Shield OS" that lives on the device.
*   **Typography**: Moving away from Overused Inter to something more distinctive and "Impeccable" (e.g., a technical but readable sans-serif).

### Design Principles
1.  **Silent by Default**: Only interrupt the user if absolutely necessary. Use progressive disclosure.
2.  **Visual Relief**: Use generous whitespace and tinted neutrals to reduce cognitive load.
3.  **Trust through Precision**: High attention to detail in alignment, spacing, and micro-interactions to signal reliability.
4.  **Tinted, Not Pure**: Avoid pure #000 or #FFF. Every color should be part of a cohesive, tinted palette.
